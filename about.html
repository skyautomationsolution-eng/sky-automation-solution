About Sky Automation Solution

Sky Automation Solution is a Philippine-based automation agency specializing in AI chatbots and workflow automation for staycation and hospitality businesses.

We help property owners automate guest communications, booking inquiries, lead management, check-ins, check-outs, and operational workflows.

Our Mission

To help hospitality businesses provide exceptional guest experiences through reliable automation technology.

Our Vision

To become a trusted automation partner for the hospitality industry across the Philippines.
